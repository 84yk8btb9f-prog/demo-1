Our Story

A passion for Mediterranean cuisine and authentic hospitality

Where Tradition Meets Excellence

Olive & Thyme was born from a simple dream: to bring the authentic flavors and warm hospitality of the Mediterranean to Athens. Founded in 2020 by Chef Dimitris Papadopoulos and his wife Maria, our restaurant is a celebration of family recipes passed down through generations, reimagined with a contemporary touch.

Every dish tells a story – from the grilled octopus that reminds Chef Dimitris of summer days in his grandfather's taverna on Paros, to the handmade dolmades that follow his grandmother's treasured recipe. We believe that food is more than sustenance; it's a connection to our roots, our culture, and each other.

Meet Our Chef

Chef Dimitris Papadopoulos

Born and raised on the island of Paros, Chef Dimitris grew up surrounded by the aromas of his grandmother's kitchen and the fresh catch from his grandfather's fishing boat. His culinary journey began at age 15 when he started working in his family's seaside taverna, where he learned that the secret to great cooking lies in respecting the ingredients and honoring tradition.

After training at Le Monde Institute of Hotel and Tourism Studies in Athens and working in Michelin-starred restaurants across Europe, Dimitris returned home with a mission: to elevate traditional Greek cuisine while staying true to its soul. At Olive & Thyme, he masterfully blends time-honored techniques with contemporary presentation, creating dishes that are both familiar and surprising.
