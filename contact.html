FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a reservation?

While walk-ins are welcome, we highly recommend making a reservation, especially for dinner and weekends, to ensure we can accommodate you.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely! We offer vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options. Please inform us of any allergies or dietary needs when making your reservation.

Is parking available?

Street parking is available in the area. We also have a partnership with a nearby parking garage that offers discounted rates for our guests.

Do you host private events?

Yes! We have a private dining room that can accommodate up to 30 guests. Contact us for details on hosting birthdays, anniversaries, corporate events, and more.

What is your dress code?

We have a smart casual dress code. While we want you to feel comfortable, we ask that guests avoid beachwear and athletic attire.

Do you offer takeaway?

Yes, we offer takeaway for most menu items. Call us directly or use popular delivery apps to place your order.
